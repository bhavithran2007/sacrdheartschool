Message from the Principal

I take this opportunity to specially thank the almighty for leading and guiding us through the thick and thin and protecting our children from all harm under adverse circumstances.We are committed to achieving excellence in all that we do. This requires dedication, determination, hard work, and a relentless approach toward achieving the very highest standards. Such a dream is possible only if we work hand in hand as parents and teachers. For every parent is the first teacher to a child, and every child is a teacher to its parents.

“Our actions speak louder than words.” If we want our children to be hard-working, let us be a model in working hard and doing our work diligently. If you do not want your children to postpone their work, then you need to reduce your screen time on your phone and fulfill the small, little promises that you make for them. In this manner, we can bring about a holistic development in the personality of our children. The journey to a long road begins with the small step. Similarly, I suggest to all parents whenever your child demands anything, do not provide it immediately; delay the gratification. Sooner or later, you will realize this delay in fulfilling their desire will bring in them qualities like tolerance, patience, a sense of responsibility, and self-contentment. So, as we begin this new academic session, let us hope and pray that this year be a fruitful one for the students as well as their teachers.

Sincerely,
Sr. Deepa Arockia Mercy.J
Principal

Life At Sacred Heart

Overall Development

Sacred Heart dedicates significant time and effort to nurturing the overall development of children, focusing on essential life skills and fostering awareness of the environment and social systems. This is achieved through initiatives such as the nature club, educational tours, eco-trips, and various other activities.

Facilities

World-class sports facilities, including volleyball courts, football grounds, kho kho and kabaddi fields, table tennis tables, badminton courts, skating rinks, and silambam arenas, are available. In addition, dedicated spaces for music and dance are provided, along with qualified trainers to help students develop their skills in sports and performing arts.

Clubs and Activities

The school offers various enriching clubs and activities, including the Eco Club, Junior Red Cross (JRC), Scouts, Tourism Club, SEWA (Social Empowerment and Welfare Association), and Art & Craft. These clubs provide students with opportunities to develop their leadership, social responsibility, and creative skills, guided by experienced mentors to encourage holistic growth and community involvement.
