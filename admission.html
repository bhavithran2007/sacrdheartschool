Admission Procedure

Sacred Heart School Mathur holds admission for children to the Pre.K.G & L.K.G class in the month of March every year.

  • Those children who attain the age of three and half years on 31st March of the same year are eligible for Pre.K.G., and four and a half years for L.K.G. admission.
  • Birth certificate and Aadhar is to be submitted at the time of admission.
  • Date of birth once entered in the school records will not be changed under any circumstance.
