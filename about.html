About Us

We Train Your Child To Their Best

Sacred Heart Sr. Sec. School, Mathur, is a Christian minority school, under the management of the Institute of Sisters of Charity of Jesus and Mary, an international congregation known as Sisters of Charity of Jesus and Mary (SCJM).
The School prepares students for the C.B.S.E (Central Board of Secondary Education) Examination i.e. Class 10, 10+2, which are recognised for admission to degree courses in the Indian universities. Besides the normal school curriculum, we also give due importance, to co-curricular and extra curricular activities. Some of these include sports, games, dance, singing, Yoga, Art and craft etc.,
